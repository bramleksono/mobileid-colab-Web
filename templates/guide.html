User Guide

General concept

This website is a platform for managing contract document and legalize agreement in electronic document.
By signing document using electronic signature, your document is legalized and protected by law.

Steps

Service provider
- Create new project.
- Invite buyer by enter their ID Number.
- Enter 1st milestone name.
Client
- Contact seller to invited in project.
After project created
- Send identity request.
- Verify others identity and send approval if you agree to begin project.
- Upload Document to current milestone.
- Sign Document by signer.
- Use milestone to organize document.
